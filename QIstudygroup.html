Quantum Information & Many-body Physics

I have founded a quantum information study group since 2024, covering various quantum information from resource theory of quantum computing to quantum information theoretic approach of many-boy physics. These are the topics we have covered so far:

  • Resource Theory of Quantum Computing: Investigating how thermodynamic principles apply to quantum systems and the role of information in thermalization processes.
  • Quantum Information meets Quantum Matter (Textbook): Studying computational models that leverage many-body interactions and topological properties for robust quantum computation.
  • Tensor Network Contractions: Methods and Applications to Quantum Many-Body Systems (Textbook): Exploring exotic phases of matter and their information-theoretic implications in condensed matter systems.
  • Entanglement and Correlations in Many-body Physics: Understanding quantum entanglement structures and their impact on quantum state complexity.
