Thermal Operations vs. Gibbs-Preserving Covariant Channels
PI ‚Äî Dr. Nelly Ng (NTU Singapore)
2024 ‚Äì 2025

We proved that, for systems with non-degenerate energy gaps, the set of states reachable under Gibbs-preserving channels that are covariant with respect to time translations coincides exactly with those reachable under Thermal Operations (TO). The work extends resource- theoretic notions of thermodynamic accessibility beyond qubit models to arbitrary finite-dimension Hilbert spaces, and gives an explicit catalytic construction showing the equivalence. Current efforts focus on the computational complexity of deciding TO-reachability and its implications for quantum thermodynamic engines.
